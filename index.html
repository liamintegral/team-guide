Account Management Team Guide

Integral Media Team Responsibilities & Procedures

Overview

As an account management team member at Integral Media, you are responsible for the successful performance and management of assigned client accounts. This guide outlines your daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly, and six-monthly responsibilities to ensure consistent, high-quality service delivery.

Your primary focus areas: Client Communication • Task Management • Performance Monitoring
Time Tracking Requirements

What to Log

  • All time spent on client-related tasks, regardless of size
  • Minimum increment: 15 minutes
  • Include time for emails, calls, content creation, reviews, research, and admin tasks
Manager Feedback - What to Log

How to Log Time

Option 1: Use Zoho Projects built-in timing features to track time as you work

Option 2: Log time manually in 15-minute increments after completing tasks

  • Always log time against the specific client and task
  • Include brief descriptions of work completed
Manager Feedback - How to Log Time

Why This Matters

Accurate Billing

Time tracking ensures transparent, accurate billing based on actual time invested

Resource Allocation

Understanding time requirements helps allocate team members and plan workload distribution

Business Intelligence

Detailed time data allows profitability analysis and improved project estimation

Daily Requirement: Log time at least once daily, ideally as you complete each task or at the end of each workday.

Weekly Responsibilities

Client Project Reviews (2x per week)

What: Comprehensive review of each assigned client's project status

Frequency: Minimum twice weekly per client (recommend Monday and Thursday)

Process:

  • Review all active tasks and projects for the client
  • Check task completion status and identify any overdue items
  • Look for "orphaned" tasks that may have been forgotten or lack clear ownership
  • Identify blocked tasks and determine next steps to unblock them
  • Use your initiative to resolve issues independently when possible
  • Collaborate with team members to move projects forward
  • Escalate only when absolutely necessary - exhaust other options first

Client Communication Standards

Client Phone Calls & Documentation

Requirements for all client calls:

Preparation:

  • Review client account status before the call
  • Prepare agenda with key discussion points
  • Have relevant performance data readily available

During the Call:

  • Take detailed notes throughout the conversation
  • Confirm understanding of any new requirements or changes
  • Set clear expectations for next steps and timelines
Post-Call Actions - Must be completed within 24 hours
  • Upload detailed notes to the CRM system
  • Create a comprehensive list of action points with owners and deadlines
  • Send follow-up email to client summarising discussion and next steps
  • Create tasks in the project management system for all action items
  • Assign tasks to appropriate team members
  • Set reminders for follow-up actions
