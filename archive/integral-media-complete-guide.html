Daily Operations Guide

Your step-by-step daily workflow for client management excellence

üåÖ YOUR DAILY WORKFLOW

Start each day with this checklist to ensure consistent client service and task management

Morning Routine (First 60-90 minutes)

  • ‚òê Check Email Accounts: Your work email and assigned partner email inbox, triage accordingly
  • ‚òê Review Personal Tasks: Review personal task list in Zoho Projects, create a plan for the day
  • ‚òê Standup with Liam: Discuss challenges, daily plan, and priorities

Ongoing Daily Activities

  • ‚òê Execute Assigned Tasks: Move along assigned tasks where possible, update progress regularly
  • ‚òê Client Project Reviews: Check each client's project 2x/week for orphaned, forgotten, or blocked tasks
  • ‚òê Performance Monitoring: Check client performance 2x/month, review services and performance trends
  • ‚òê Client Communications: Complete scheduled calls with full documentation and follow-up

üìß EMAIL & TASK TRIAGE PROCEDURES

Email Processing Workflow

  • Step 1: Categorize email (Client Request, Internal Communication, FYI, Emergency)
  • Step 2: Determine urgency (Immediate response, Same day, Within 24 hours)
  • Step 3: Create Zoho Projects task if actionable work is required
  • Step 4: Send acknowledgment response with timeline
  • Step 5: Update CRM with communication log

Task Prioritization System

  • Priority 1: Client emergencies or urgent requests
  • Priority 2: Tasks with today's deadline
  • Priority 3: Revenue-impacting activities (campaigns, reporting)
  • Priority 4: Content creation and approval workflows
  • Priority 5: Administrative and documentation tasks

üìã CLIENT PROJECT MANAGEMENT

Project Review Checklist (2x per week)

  • Review all active tasks for each assigned client project
  • Identify orphaned tasks (no assignee or unclear ownership)
  • Check for blocked tasks (waiting on client input, dependencies)
  • Verify task deadlines are realistic and achievable
  • Use initiative to move tasks along with wider team support
  • Escalate only when necessary after attempting resolution

Performance Review Process (2x per month)

  • Review service performance across all platforms and campaigns
  • Check performance trends to ensure positive direction
  • Identify performance issues early before they impact client satisfaction
  • Liaise with appropriate teams (SEO, PPC, Social) to address issues
  • Document findings and action items in client CRM
  • Proactive communication with clients about improvements or concerns

üìû CLIENT COMMUNICATION WORKFLOW

Call Preparation & Execution

  • Pre-call: Review recent performance data and project status
  • During call: Take detailed notes using structured format
  • Post-call: Upload notes to CRM within 1 hour
  • Action items: Create specific tasks in Zoho Projects
  • Follow-up: Send summary email with next steps

Call Documentation Requirements

  • Call date, time, and participants
  • Key discussion points and client feedback
  • Performance highlights and concerns raised
  • Action items with assigned owners and deadlines
  • Next meeting date and agenda items
  • Client satisfaction indicators and relationship health

üîß DAILY TASK MANAGEMENT

Task Execution Best Practices

  • Acknowledge immediately: Respond to new task assignments within 30 minutes
  • Ask questions early: Clarify requirements before starting work
  • Update progress regularly: Add comments throughout the day showing progress
  • Adjust deadlines proactively: If delays anticipated, update timeline immediately
  • Document completion: Include summary of work completed when marking done

End-of-Day Checklist

  • Update all active tasks with current status in Zoho Projects
  • Plan tomorrow's priorities based on deadlines and client needs
  • Clear email inbox or defer non-urgent items to tomorrow
  • Document any issues or blockers for standup discussion
  • Review calendar for tomorrow's meetings and preparation needed

    Client Risk Assessment System

    Comprehensive framework for evaluating client risk and opportunity factors

    üîç Risk Assessment Matrix

    Financial Risk Factors

    • Payment History: Track record of timely payments
    • Budget Stability: Consistency of monthly spend
    • Contract Terms: Length and termination clauses
    • Industry Volatility: Sector-specific economic factors
    • Business Size: Revenue stability and growth trajectory

    Operational Risk Factors

    • Communication Style: Responsiveness and clarity
    • Decision Making: Speed and consistency of approvals
    • Technical Requirements: Complexity of implementation
    • Team Changes: Frequency of contact person turnover
    • Scope Creep: Tendency to request additional work

    Growth Opportunity Factors

    • Service Expansion: Potential for additional services
    • Budget Growth: Likelihood of increased investment
    • Referral Potential: Network and recommendation value
    • Case Study Value: Success story and marketing potential
    • Innovation Openness: Willingness to try new strategies

    Relationship Quality Factors

    • Trust Level: Confidence in our recommendations
    • Collaboration: Willingness to work as partners
    • Feedback Quality: Constructive and specific input
    • Long-term Vision: Alignment with strategic goals
    • Cultural Fit: Values and working style compatibility

    üìä Risk Scoring System

    Risk Category Low Risk (1-3) Medium Risk (4-6) High Risk (7-10) Weight
    Financial Stability Excellent payment history, stable budget Occasional delays, budget fluctuations Payment issues, unstable finances 30%
    Communication Quality Responsive, clear, professional Sometimes slow, unclear requirements Poor communication, unrealistic expectations 25%
    Project Complexity Standard services, clear scope Some custom requirements Highly complex, changing requirements 20%
    Market Conditions Stable industry, growing market Some market volatility Declining industry, economic pressure 15%
    Relationship Health Strong partnership, mutual trust Professional but distant Strained relationship, frequent conflicts 10%
